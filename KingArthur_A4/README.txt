A4 Vroom Vroom! Outrun the Faeries

This program lets you drive a weird unicycle like vehicle through a grid. Now a cute teapot follows a predefined curve around you!
Press w/s to move forward and back.
Press a/d to turn left/right
To move the camera, click and drag the cursor on the screen.
To zoom in and out, click and drag the cursor on the screen while holding down either control key.
To toggle the control cage, hit "c".
To toggle the faerie curve, hit "b".
compile me by typing 'make'
run the assignment with a control point file
